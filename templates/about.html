Our Journey

A small idea that kept growing.

From a weekend hackathon project to a tool used by tens of thousands — here's how MyTodo came to be.

2020 — Weekend Hackathon

The First Version

Two developers, one weekend, zero sleep. A raw but functional todo app built in Django to scratch their own itch — tracking tasks without features they'd never need.

2021 — Open Beta

First 1,000 Users

After sharing on a developer forum, MyTodo attracted its first thousand users within a week. Feedback shaped priority tagging, categories, and due-date reminders.

2022 — Design Overhaul

Beautiful Gets Useful

A complete redesign made MyTodo feel as good as it worked. The warm earthy palette and Plus Jakarta Sans typeface became the app's identity.

2023 — 25k Users

Word of Mouth Takes Over

No ads. No growth hacks. Just people sharing a tool they genuinely loved — students, freelancers, remote teams, and small businesses alike.

2024 — Today

50k+ and Counting

MyTodo is used in 30+ countries. The mission stays the same: help you spend less time managing tasks and more time actually doing them.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

MyTodo is intentionally lean. Every feature earns its place by making your workflow genuinely better.

Instant Task Creation

Hit Enter and it's done. Add title, description, priority, category and due date in one smooth form — no popups, no friction.

Smart Prioritization

High, medium, and low priority labels with colour-coded visual cues so the most important work always stands out at a glance.

Categories & Filters

Organize tasks by Personal, Work, Shopping, Health or Study. Filter instantly — no page reloads, no waiting.

Due Date Tracking

Tasks show "Today" or "Overdue" automatically so nothing slips through the cracks. Your deadlines are always visible.

Live Search

Type in the search bar and results highlight in real time across titles and descriptions. Find any task in under a second.

Offline Storage

All your tasks are saved to localStorage — no account needed, no internet required. Your data, your device, your privacy.

Is MyTodo really free?

Yes — 100% free, forever. Core task management will never go behind a paywall. We believe everyone deserves great productivity tools regardless of budget.

Do I need an account to use it?

No account needed. Your tasks are saved directly to your browser's localStorage. Open the app and start adding tasks — no sign-up, no email, no password required.

Will my tasks disappear if I clear my browser?

If you clear your browser's site data, yes — localStorage is wiped. We recommend exporting your tasks regularly. A cloud sync feature is on our roadmap for 2025.

Is MyTodo built with Django?

The frontend templates use Django's templating engine (django-html). The full backend is Python + Django with a REST API. The current demo runs as a standalone HTML file using localStorage.

Can I contribute to MyTodo?

Absolutely! MyTodo is open source. Fork the repo on GitHub, open an issue, or submit a pull request. All contributions are welcome — code, design, docs, or bug reports.

What's coming next?

On the roadmap: cloud sync, recurring tasks, drag-and-drop reordering, dark mode, and a mobile app. Follow us on GitHub to track progress and vote on features.
